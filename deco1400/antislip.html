Anti-Slip Coating

Are you concerned about falling in you reglazed bathtub, and prefer the look and feel of an anti-slip surface that’s part of the tub itself? Not interested in a safety mat? Bath Magic can as part of the reglazing process, can apply an anti-slip application for bathtub bottoms to prevent accidents. Our system is called Stand-Fast™ and it can be installed during the bathtub refinishing process. Once installed Stand-Fast™ is part of the refinished bathtub and will continue to offer protection for the life of the refinished tub.
